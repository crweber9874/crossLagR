3.1 Summary

The change (LCM) and level score (CLPM; RICLPM) analysis are equivalent in the absense of unit effects. This suggests a close relationship between these models. However, when unit effects are present, the models diverge. When the DGP is a latent change model with constant effects, the CLPM and RICLPM retrieve incorrect estimates

CLPM retrieves the incorrect parameter estimates. “Unit Effects” themselves are also not always comparable, with unit average change score forming the LCMs unit effects, and unit average level score forming the RICLPMs unit effects.
